My Work

The Impact of Foreign Exchange Rate on GDP: The Case of Ghana.

This research investigated the impact of foreign exchange rate on GDP growth in Ghana using annual time series data. I tested for stationarity of the data using the Augmented Dicky-Fuller test(ADF). I employed the multiple regression analysis to assess the impact taking into consideration other important macroeconomic variables. For further tests, I used the Vector Autoregression Mode(VAR) with main focus on the Granger Causality as well as the impulse functions. The granger causality was used as a further test to determine if the independent variable can predict the dependent variable and vice versa.
